Adventures in Fire Island

Fire Island is a very long, thin and diverse barrier island. It is located in New York City, northeast USA. Finest beaches are on the western part, serene isolated nature on the eastern part, having huge rambling dunes, clear ocean views and cattails. The central is most visited, full of life making it famous. Enjoyed boat riding, skiing and interacted with different communities with unique diversified cultures.
